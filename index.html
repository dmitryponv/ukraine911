This page is created for our humanitarian relief organization. Currently, all our volunteering work is funded by ourselves with personal income and donations we've received from generous volunteers (shown below), However, more funding can provide additional aid, which we cannot fund with a paycheck.

Below is a record of what we have finished so far at our own expense and what we am looking to do with funds provided by donations. With the current income we will continue at this steady pace, but more can be accomplished with additional funding.

We will update this page every week and manually enter all donors as donations arrive. Any amount or sharing is appreciated. All donations will 100% go toward purchasing supplies, humanitarian aid, housing, or other donations. We can provide receipts and photos to anyone who needs. Thank you all.
